About Our Restaurant

Country Cheese & Wine is our heart-project. We dreamt about opening this place for years. We wanted to provide a place in Târgu Mureș that introduces a spin on traditional food and surprises our guests with innovative cooking forms. It's our mission to enhance and educate the palate with the freshest ingredients and flavours, while indulging and exciting each guest with personal care and special service. After facing many obstacles, we were finally able to open up our place in August 2021. We cannot wait to welcome you!
